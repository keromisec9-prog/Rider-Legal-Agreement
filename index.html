DuukaExpress Rider Legal Agreement

Terms, Safety, Fraud & Privacy Policy

A. Terms & Conditions

Acceptance of Agreement

By registering or using DuukaExpress, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old, all information provided is accurate, and you agree to all platform policies. Enforcement is based on defined system rules, not arbitrary decisions.

Independent Contractor Relationship

Riders are independent service providers, not employees of DuukaExpress. Riders control their working hours and earnings are not guaranteed.

Platform Role

DuukaExpress provides a technology platform connecting customers and riders. It does not operate vehicles or control physical delivery execution.

Commission & Fees

A service commission is applied to each completed job and recorded in the rider account. Riders must settle outstanding commission periodically based on platform rules.

Enforcement Policy

Account actions are based on verified system data including fraud checks, payment status, and safety compliance.

Account States

  • Active — full access
  • Restricted — limited access due to minor issues
  • Suspended — under investigation
  • Terminated — permanent closure for severe violations

Liability Limitation

DuukaExpress is not responsible for accidents, loss, theft, or damages occurring during service execution. Riders assume full responsibility for their operations.

B. Safety Policy

Obligations

  • Follow all traffic and transport laws
  • Wear required safety gear
  • Ensure vehicle is roadworthy
  • Refuse unsafe or illegal deliveries

Prohibited Actions

  • Driving under alcohol or drugs
  • Reckless driving
  • Overloading vehicles
  • Transporting illegal goods
Safety violations may result in warning, suspension, or permanent termination depending on severity.

C. Fraud & Abuse Policy

Definition

  • Fake identity or documents
  • Duplicate accounts
  • GPS or earnings manipulation
  • Unauthorized account use

Detection Methods

Fraud detection is based on system logs, identity verification data, device fingerprinting, and behavioral analysis.

Enforcement Process

Accounts may be flagged, suspended for investigation, or permanently terminated based on evidence.

Consequences

  • Warning
  • Temporary suspension
  • Permanent ban
  • Device and identity blacklisting

Appeals

Users may appeal decisions through official channels within 3–7 business days, unless permanently blacklisted due to confirmed fraud.

D. Privacy Policy

Data Collected

  • Identity information (name, ID number)
  • Contact information
  • Vehicle details
  • Location during active use
  • Verification images

Purpose

  • Verification
  • Fraud prevention
  • Service operation
  • Safety enforcement

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for operational and legal requirements. Suspended or banned accounts may be retained for security enforcement purposes.

Data Protection

Data is stored securely with restricted access to authorized personnel only.

Data Sharing

DuukaExpress does not sell personal data. Data may only be shared when required by law or fraud investigations.
